We also offer

Homework Supervision & Aftercare

A calm, guided environment for learning beyond school hours designed to support school-going learners.

Swimming

Swimming lessons offered as part of physical development and supports water safety, confidence, and motor skills. Participation subject to applicable swimming fees.

Digital Learning, Coding & Robotics

We introduce leaners to early digital literacy and computational thinking through coding & robotics lessons in a fun and age-appropriate way.

Making & Baking Programme

Learners are given opportunities to prepare and make simple food items under supervision. This programme fosters independence and confidence while encouraging hands-on learning, introducing basic food preparation, hygiene, and nutrition concepts, and supporting fine motor development through guided instruction.

Weekend Care(Additional Service)

Weekend care available at an additional cost. Ideal for working parents who require supervised care outside normal weekday hours. Must be arranged in advance.

School Transport

Transport available within Cosmo City and Northriding areas. Safe, supervised collection and drop-off for enrolled learners.

Our Commitment

Our school ethos is grounded in faith, excellence, community, and independence. We are committed to creating a nurturing and inclusive environment where every child feels valued, safe, and encouraged to grow. Through a balanced, holistic approach to learning, we support each learner’s academic, social, emotional, and moral development, helping children become confident, caring, and capable individuals who are prepared for the next stage of their educational journey.
